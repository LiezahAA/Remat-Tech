Our singular purpose

Building trust in Africa's
trade infrastructure

At Remat Technologies, we are driven by a singular purpose: to build trust in Africa's trade infrastructure. We empower businesses to trade globally with confidence by automating treasury operations and simplifying their cross-border trade payments.

Founded in 2017, Remat Technologies is a licensed treasury management and cross-border infrastructure provider with operational bases in the United Kingdom, Canada, and Nigeria and a strong presence across Sub-Saharan Africa.
