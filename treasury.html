Real-time visibility, control, and automation over your financial operations with Remat Technologies Automated Treasury Management Solution — designed for Chief Financial Officers, Treasurers, and Corporate Finance teams managing complex, cross-border transactions.

Features

Built as Africa's first end-to-end treasury and payment infrastructure, our platform empowers enterprises with:

Multi-Bank Connectivity

Seamlessly connect to multiple banks through a single interface. Access aggregated account balances and statements across all your banking relationships, while enabling centralised payments to improve efficiency, visibility, and control of your cash operations.

SWIFT for Corporates (MT Messages)

Leverage secured communication channels using SWIFT MT messages for direct interaction with your banks. Enjoy end-to-end control over your bank accounts through ERP automation and reconciliation, ensuring speed, accuracy, and compliance in your treasury operations.

Cash & Liquidity Management

Access a real-time, consolidated view of cash positions across entities and currencies. Forecast inflows and outflows accurately, drive informed decision-making, and ensure your business maintains optimal liquidity and strategic financial agility.

Tiered Approval Workflows

Configure flexible, role-based approval limits across your organisation. Maintain robust internal controls while empowering teams to move quickly, with full audit trails for every transaction and approval decision.
