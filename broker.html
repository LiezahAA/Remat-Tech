Built for Broker-Dealers and Capital Market Firms

Communication Is the Compliance Risk
Most Capital Market Firms Haven't Solved Yet.

Broker-dealers and capital market firms operate in one of the most communication-intensive, compliance-sensitive environments in financial services. Our Secure Enterprise Communication and Workflow Platform was built for exactly this reality.

The Challenge

Fragmented, Unarchived Communication Is a Compliance Risk You're Already Carrying.

Traders are calling counterparties on personal phones. Deals are being discussed and confirmed on WhatsApp. Internal coordination happens across a mix of messaging apps, emails, and informal channels. None of it is captured. None of it is searchable. And if a dispute arises or a regulator asks you to have no record and no defence. The global regulatory direction on off-channel communications is unambiguous. The time to get ahead of it is now.

Compliant Trader Voice and Messaging

Replace fragmented communication with a single, secure, fully recorded platform. Every call, every message, every counterparty interaction captured, archived, and searchable. All from a mobile-first interface built for how traders actually work.

WhatsApp and Multi-Channel Compliance
