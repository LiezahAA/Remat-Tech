Frequently Asked
Questions

01
What is the main benefit of Remat Technologies's Treasury Management Platform?
It gives finance teams a single, real-time view of balances and cash positions across every market and currency, with automated reconciliation built in.
02
How does the platform handle compliance and regulatory reporting?
Remat Technologies runs automated screening and reporting workflows tailored to each jurisdiction we operate in, so your business stays compliant by default.
03
How does the platform handle currency conversion and transactions?
Conversions run on transparent, real-time FX rates with no hidden markups, and settlement happens directly through local payment rails.
04
What makes Remat Technologies' approach to cross-border transaction management different?
A single integration connects you to a growing network of banks and mobile money providers across Africa, removing the need for multiple local accounts.
05
Which countries and currencies does Remat Technologies currently support?
Coverage spans major African markets with continual expansion, supporting both local and major global currencies for payments and settlement.
06
How does Remat Technologies support enterprises with high transaction volumes?
Dedicated infrastructure, priority settlement queues and account management ensure enterprise volumes are processed reliably at scale.
