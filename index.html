Frequently Asked
Questions

01
What does Remat Technologies do?
We identify, partner with, and deploy globally proven technology platforms to organisations across West Africa. Our current portfolio spans financial services, compliance, data intelligence, workforce development, and digital operations.
02
Which sectors does Remat Technologies serve?
Our current deployments are concentrated in financial services like banks, capital market firms, lenders, and development finance institutions. But our mandate is broader than any single sector. We go where the gap between world-class technology and institutional capability is widest.
03
Does Remat Technologies develop its own software?
We are a deployment and partnership firm, not a software developer. We bring globally proven platforms to West African organisations, configure and implement them to local requirements, and provide ongoing support and account management. The platforms we deploy are in active use at some of the world's leading institutions.
04
Where does Remat Technologies operate?
We are headquartered in Accra, Ghana, with a mandate across West Africa. Our immediate focus is Ghana's financial services sector, with active expansion plans into Nigeria and the broader region
05
How do I find out which platform is right for my organisation?
Start a conversation with us. We scope every engagement carefully. We will listen to what you are trying to solve, tell you honestly whether we have a solution that fits, and take it from there
