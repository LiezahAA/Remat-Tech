Expanding across borders shouldn't mean navigating complex financial barriers. Our Cross Border Financial Infrastructure (CBFI) is built to remove friction, enabling businesses, fintechs, and remittance providers to operate seamlessly across global markets — with the efficiency of a local player.

Features

Through an API-driven, secure, and regulatory-compliant platform, we empower organisations with:

Treasury & Account Structure

  • Operate across multiple jurisdictions.
  • Centralised monitoring and control.
  • Automated reconciliation and reporting.

Multi-Currency, Client-Named Accounts

  • Instantly set up accounts in your own name, not Remat Technologies.
  • Hold and manage multiple currencies across different jurisdictions.
  • Achieve full transparency and operational control over cross-border funds.

Effortless Local Currency Collections & Funding

  • Collect and deposit local currencies without the complexities of correspondent banking.
  • Support for ACH, mobile money, local bank transfers, and more.
  • Optimised for fintechs, global businesses, and international service providers.

Real-Time FX & Competitive Rates

  • Access live foreign exchange rates across major African and global currencies.
  • Execute conversions instantly with transparent pricing.
  • Lock in rates for future transactions to manage FX exposure.

Cross-Border Payment Infrastructure

  • Send and receive funds across 20+ African markets and globally.
  • Direct connectivity to local payment rails and SWIFT.
  • Real-time settlement with full transaction visibility.
