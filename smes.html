Commercial Banks Are Operating Under More Pressure Than Ever.

Commercial banks today are managing more regulatory obligation, more credit risk exposure, and more communication complexity than at any point in the past decade. What Remat Delivers for Commercial Banks

Treasury and Balance Sheet Control

Real-time deal capture, ALM modelling, and automated regulatory reporting giving your treasury and risk teams the visibility and tools to manage complexity confidently.

End-to-End Lending Automation

From loan origination through to IFRS 9 provisioning, automate the full credit lifecycle. Reduce manual processing, sharpen credit decisions, and keep your NPL position firmly in check.

Regulated Communication Infrastructure

Your relationship managers are communicating with clients on channels that aren't being captured or monitored. Our platform brings every conversation into a compliant, auditable environment without disrupting how your teams work.
